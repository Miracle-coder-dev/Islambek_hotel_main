Discover the Aral Sea in 2 days and 1 night


  • Route

    Khiva - Urgench - Chilpik Kala - Nukus Art Museum - Muynak Ship Cemetery - Aral Sea Museum - Aral Sea - Great Ustyurt Canyons - Mizdakhan Necropolis - Urgench - Khiva

  • Details

    Duration: 2 days and 1 night
    Driving time: 16-18 hours
    Sightseeing time: 6-8 hours
    Daytrip length : 1050 km.
    Included: Fuel, Driver & Drinking Water
    Not Included: Yurt Camp stay, Entrance tickets (Chilpik Kala is 10,000 sums, Nukus Museum is 80,500 sums and Muynak Aral Sea museum is 30,000 sums for each tourist), meals & etc.

  • Price

    1 person - $340

    2 people - $350

    3 people - $360

    4 people - $370

  • How to Reserve

    Please provide and confirm following details via E-mail or Whatsapp to make a reservation:
    1. Name of the Daytrip/Transfer.
    2. Date of the Daytrip/Transfer.
    3. Number of Participants.
    4. Full names.
    5. Pick up and starting time.
    6. Pick up and starting place.
    7. Drop off place
    That is all we need to know from your side in advance to book and organize our Daytrips and Transfers.

  • Daytrip start

    It is recommended to start the trip around 6 a.m. from your hotel in Khiva

  • Itinerary

    Day 1:
    06:00 - start the trip.
    07:30 - 08:00 – visit Tower of Silence – Chilpik Kala.
    09:00 – 10:30 – visit Nukus Savitskiy Art Museum,
    13:00 – 15:00 visit Muynak Ship Cemetery and Muynak Museum. Change the sedan car to 4WD.
    18:30 – arriving to the shore of the Aral Sea. There are Yurt Camps and Tents available to sleep which is charged around $20 per person. Please enjoy swimming in the salty water of the Sea, hiking on the hills or making fire.

    Day 2:
    07:00 - 09:00 – time to look around, walking in the sea shore and taking nice pictures of the sunrise.
    10:30 - 11:00 – visit Great Ustyurt Canyons & Tombs of the nomad fishermen’s.
    12:30 - 13:00 – arriving Muynak and change 4WD to Sedan (previous car car).
    15:30 - 16:00 – visiting architectural complex Mizdakhan Necropolis from XIV century.
    19:00 – arriving Khiva and we end the trip in your hotel, train station or Airport.
